WDI Repository Naming Convention:

See IMG_1298.JPG and IMG_1299.JPG for original notes.

Should be all lowercase characters and hyphens as separators.

(language|framework)-topic-[type]-[sequence]

Examples:
js-fundamentals
js-fundamentals-assessment
jquery-dom-manipulation
rails-
ruby-
node-
express-
sql-
mongo-
mongoose-

types:
assessment, practice, lab, template, project.
(Do not use demo, quiz, homework or lesson)
